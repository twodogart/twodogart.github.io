About the artist
Gail Hunter is a GARNA award winning photographer whose photos have appeared in her local newspaper, The Mountain Mail. A geographer and nature enthusiast, her photography is not edited. She relies solely on her skill of embracing the natural light to capture images. The magical colors of her work are often found during her favorite daily ritual, walking her dogs to the songs of the sunrise. As a former runner, Gail now enjoys a slower pace which allows time for photos. Gail is a retired attorney, the mother of four children and grandmother of five, and lives in Salida, Colorado.
